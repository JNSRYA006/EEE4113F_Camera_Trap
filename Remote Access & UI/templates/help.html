Camera Trap Dashboard Help

About the Dashboard

Welcome to the Camera Trap Dashboard, your gateway to the mesmerizing world of wildlife captured by your camera trap! Immerse yourself in the wonders of nature as you remotely access the stunning images and last recorded localized climate data, all from the comfort of your own device.

Explore the hidden corners of your wildlife sanctuary, witness rare and elusive creatures in their natural habitats, and embark on a visual journey filled with awe and inspiration. With the Camera Trap Dashboard, every image becomes a window to a world brimming with biodiversity and captivating moments.

But it doesn't stop there! Our dashboard goes beyond visual delights. It empowers you with valuable insights into the battery life and Raspberry Pi configuration of your camera trap, ensuring you have a comprehensive overview of its operational status. Stay informed and connected to the heartbeat of your wildlife sanctuary like never before.

Whether you are a wildlife enthusiast, a conservationist, or simply an explorer at heart, the Camera Trap Dashboard invites you to embark on a virtual adventure, where every click brings you closer to the untamed beauty of the natural world.

Functionalities

Image Retrieval

Last Three Images

View the last three images that were captured. Click the button to retrieve the last three captured images.

Images from a Date

View images from the specified date. Select a date and then click the button to see the images that were captured on the date you specified.

Images at a Specific Time

View three images taken within a specific time range. Select a time and then click the button to view images that were taken around the time you specified.

The retrieved images will be displayed in this section. You can browse through them and witness the captivating wildlife moments captured by your camera trap.

Climate Data Retrieval

Last Three Climate Measurements

View the last three climate measurements recorded. Click the button to retrieve the last three measured readings.

Climate Data From Date

View climate measurements recorded on a specific date. Select a date and then click the button to retrieve the recorded measurements on the specified day.

Climate Data at a Specific Time

View climate measurements taken within a specific time range. Select a time and then click the button to retrieve measurements recorded around the specified time.

The retrieved climate data will be displayed in this section. You can explore the temperature, humidity, and other relevant environmental measurements recorded by your camera trap. Gain insights into the local climate conditions and their impact on wildlife behavior and habitats.

Battery Life and Raspberry Pi Information

View Battery Life

Check the current battery status of your camera trap.

View Raspberry Pi Information

Access details about the Raspberry Pi configuration running your camera trap.

In this section, you can find important information about the battery life of your camera trap, ensuring you stay informed about its power source. Additionally, you can explore the Raspberry Pi details, including its version, connectivity, and other relevant specifications.
